The length of the bobsleigh track is 1500-2000 m, with 15 turns with a minimum radius of 8 m. During the descent, the bob can reach speeds of about 135 km / h in just 60 seconds, while the athletes experience four times the force of gravity.

Bob's crew consists of a pilot (manager) and a brakeman (sitting at the back). The team of four also includes two overclockers. Modern bobsleigh sleds are made of a streamlined all-metal body (to ensure the aerodynamics of the projectile), to which two pairs of skate runners are attached. The front pair of skates is movable and connected to the steering wheel, and the rear pair, equipped with a brake, is stationary.

Бобы бывают двух- и четырехместные, а вес находящегося внутри экипажа лимитирован 200 кг для двоих и 400 кг – для четверых. Шипы на подошвах обуви для бобслея, не должны быть толще 1 мм, длиннее 4 мм и располагаться не ближе 3 мм друг от друга. Каждая команда должна совершить 4 заезда. После окончания гонок результаты суммируются и победителями становятся обладатели наименьшего общего времени заездов. От каждой страны на Играх может выступать не более двух экипажей.

The first bobsleigh club was founded in 1897 in St. Moritz, and the first specialized tracks appeared in 1908-10. in Austria and Germany. In 1923, the International Bobsleigh and Toboggan Federation was founded. Four bobsledders first slid down the Olympic track in 1924 in Chamonix, and two in 1932.

Interesting fact:

The cost of one bob-four, together with spare parts and spare axles, is about 100,000 euros. A set of skates costs 10,000 euros. The average cost of one training descent on a good track is 55 euros for a four and 35 for a deuce.
